About

A small handmade space built around calm details.

This brand is personal, simple, and slow by design. Each piece is made by hand, with soft colours and a lot of care.

How this began

Little Loops began with a passionate homemaker who slowly turned a love for handmade art into a small business of her own.

What started as a quiet creative joy at home grew into something more meaningful over time. Crochet became a way to turn patience, care, and creativity into pieces that could be shared with others.

The heart behind it

This is still a small and personal handmade space. Every piece carries the feeling of home, soft colours, careful work, and the wish to make something that feels special when it reaches someone.

Along with keychains, bookmarks, baby items, accessories, coasters, bags, cushions, and cup holders, custom and bulk orders are also taken up with prior notice.
